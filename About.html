About

No matter how picky you are, we will cater to your needs

At Picky-Pan, we all arrive to work with a healthy, prosperous mindset. We are a newly emerged business that was started up in August 2018. One of the biggest problems out there is that there are not many websites that provide you with the ability to filter out certain food options to receive ingredients, nutritional values, and total meal preparation time. This has created a lot of opportunity for us and a desirable mission. We believe that there are more proficient ways to market the consumers of the food industry, specifically targeting those with dietary restrictions. A few of us have dietary restrictions ourselves, thus allowing us to be more passionate when it comes to designing an application to target others with similar needs. We are excited for everyone to experience our community and most importantly our software.
